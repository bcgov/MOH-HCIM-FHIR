            <li>There are no source records in the entity with an overlay task</li>

                    <li>The subscriber has a source record in the entity</li>

                    <li>Date of Death only: subscribe to MOH and MOH_VSA</li>
                    <li>Date of Death & Indicator "Y": subscribe to MOH_VSA only</li>
                </ul>
            <li>If the source has permission to provide the date of death to the composite view, the record cannot be a singleton; the member must be in an entity with a MOH_CRS record</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
