Resource Profile: BCPatient

Defining URL:http://hlth.gov.bc.ca/fhir/client/StructureDefinition/bc-patient
Version:0.1.0
Name:BCPatient
Status:Active as of 11/29/21, 6:20 PM
Definition:

General constraints on the Patient resource for use in the BC Client Registry project.

Publisher:BC Ministry of Health
Source Resource:XML / JSON / Turtle

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hlth.gov.bc.ca/fhir/client/StructureDefinition/bc-patient

Patient is a core resource of the Client Registry FHIR implementation. Patients are returned by queries (Find Candidates and Get Demographics) and modified or updated by revise and merge operations. This profile primarily adds extensions to elements that the core Patient resource does not include such as business dates.
